Indonesian Culture

Arts and culture in Indonesia are a reflection of ethnic, religious, language, and rich traditions from more than 17,000 islands that stretch from Sabang to Merauke. This diversity creates a very rich and diverse cultural heritage and influences all aspects of the daily lives of Indonesian people.
